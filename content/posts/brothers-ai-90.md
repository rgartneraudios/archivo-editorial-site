## ABSTRACT

BROTHERS AI Version 9.0 introduces significant hardware and connectivity improvements to the ecosystem. This version presents two redesigned devices: **Halo-BroPhone** (evolution of the medallion concept) and **Tiara-BroCam** (with aluminum band design), along with a comprehensive connectivity architecture that includes VISION modes (1, 1B, 2, 3) and **BROVISION LINK** for private communications. A critical safety consideration is addressed: these hands-free, voice-activated devices significantly reduce accidents caused by smartphone screen distraction, both for pedestrians and drivers.

### 2. TIARA-BROCAM (REDESIGNED - ALUMINUM BAND)

### 3. SAFETY CONSIDERATION: ACCIDENT PREVENTION

{{< figure src="/archivo-editorial-site/images/SG02.png" alt="City tour" title="City tour" >}}

**Critical Advantage Over Smartphones:**

Both **Tiara-BroCam** and **Halo-BroPhone** are designed to **eliminate screen distraction**, a leading cause of accidents for both pedestrians and drivers.

**Problem with Traditional Smartphones:**
- Looking down at a screen while walking causes collisions, falls, and traffic accidents
- Drivers using smartphones cause thousands of fatalities annually
- "Smartphone zombies" (pedestrians absorbed in screens) are a growing urban safety concern

**BROCAM Devices Solution:**
- ‚úÖ **Hands-free operation:** No need to hold or look at a device
- ‚úÖ **Voice-activated:** Interaction via natural conversation with BROHOME
- ‚úÖ **Audio-first interface:** Information delivered through headphones (Tiara-BroCam)
- ‚úÖ **Optional display:** Halo-BroPhone screen only when stationary
- ‚úÖ **Heads-up awareness:** Users maintain environmental awareness
- ‚úÖ **VISION MODE 3 (Backend):** Navigation and assistance without any screen

**Impact:**
- **Pedestrians:** Can navigate, communicate, and access information while maintaining full visual awareness of their surroundings
- **Drivers:** Can receive navigation, calls, and information via audio without taking eyes off the road
- **Public safety:** Reduced collisions, falls, and distraction-related accidents
- **Accessibility:** Particularly beneficial for visually impaired users and elderly populations

This design philosophy aligns with BROTHERS AI's core principle of technology that enhances human capability without compromising safety or presence in the physical world.
