Introduction: From the Federated to the Collaborative Concept#

In the first version of Brothers AI, we introduced the concept of Federated Artificial Intelligence: an ecosystem where intelligence did not reside in a giant corporate server, but was distributed among users, devices, and verifiable sources. It was a proposal for decentralization, privacy, and veracity.

However, as we delved deeper into the architecture, we discovered that the term “federated” did not completely capture the essence of the system. Brothers AI is not simply a network of autonomous nodes working independently. It is a machine of specialized collaborative intelligences that converse with each other, complement one another, and work together with specific roles.

For this reason, Brothers AI 2.0 adopts a new conceptual name: Collaborative Artificial Intelligence.

The Machine of Lightweight AIs
Imagine the scene in Terminator where the T-800 calls Sarah Connor‚Äôs house, and on the other end of the line… another Terminator answers. Two intelligent machines conversing with each other, recognizing one another, collaborating.

Brothers AI 2.0 functions in a similar way: they are multiple lightweight AIs (based on efficient models like Deepseek) communicating with each other. Each one knows its specific function and knows when to intervene, when to delegate, and when to protect the user.

It is not a monolithic GPT-4 attempting to do everything. It is a specialized team where each member knows exactly what they are doing.

3. ISU USER: Lego Encrypted Memory#

Advantages of the Lego System

  5. Unique Encryption per User: Each BROTHER generates its own dictionary upon creation. Patrick’s code 01 does not mean the same as Julia’s 01. If one ISU User is decrypted, others remain safe.
