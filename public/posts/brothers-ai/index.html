BROTHERS AI: Collaborative Artificial Intelligence (Concept)#

By RGartner

This past weekend, I found myself reflecting on Ilya Sutskever’s words regarding the future of Artificial Intelligence and the path toward AGI (Artificial General Intelligence). For those who follow my essays on society and philosophy, you know that my approach is usually not about code, but structural. I seek residential, logistical, or social solutions. And observing the current state of AI‚Äîgigantic models, energetically unsustainable, that hallucinate data and demand our privacy in exchange for services‚ÄîI realized that the problem isn’t a lack of power, it’s a design flaw.

Today I want to present “Brothers” to you. It’s not a new version of GPT. It’s a cognitive architecture project designed to be efficient, private, and above all, truthful. An ecosystem where intelligence doesn’t reside on a giant server, but is distributed among us.

1. BROTHER (Brother One User): The Sovereign Soul#

The current industry attempts to create a “digital God” in the cloud. Brothers proposes the opposite: an AI that lives on your device (mobile, AR glasses, computer). It’s called BROTHER.

BROTHER is a conversational language model AI without integrated Search or Thinking. Its job isn’t to know quantum physics, but to know you. It converses with you naturally and fluidly, like Gemini Live, ChatGPT, or Claude AI.

Technology and Deployment#

Depending on available technology, BROTHER can function:

  • Ideal: Within the device (on-device), leveraging neural processors in modern smartphones.
  • Initial alternative: As a cloud-based application, similar to Deepseek, GPT, Claude AI, or Gemini Live, until local technology is sufficiently mature.

Conversation History#

Conversation history will be available in the cloud only at the user’s request and through premium plans. However, extensive conversations are not recommended to avoid BROTHER hallucinations. The system’s philosophy is to maintain specific and concise conversations, letting persistent memory reside in ISU User.

Intelligent Extraction: ISU User (Information Source User)#

BROTHER has the ability to extract relevant information from conversations to feed the ISU User (Information Source User), which is the user’s private memory. Extracted data is encrypted, anonymous, and structured as necessary data to know the user’s life.

Examples of extracted data (traditional method):

  • Grandma knows how to cook lentil stew
  • Grandma goes to yoga
  • Grandma goes to yoga on Wednesdays
  • Grandpa is self-employed
  • Grandpa plays soccer
  • Bubby is a pet dog
  • Bubby barks at mail
  • Bubby barks at people
  • User sad Wednesday November 2025
  • User sad Wednesday December 2025

Advanced Experimental Alternative: Loose Words System#

As an evolution of the ISU User system, a fragmented word architecture is proposed that offers greater security and efficiency:

How it works: Instead of storing structured sentences, ISU User would only save loose keywords without explicit relationships:

grandma, yoga, wednesday, stew, lentils, grandpa, self-employed, soccer,
Bubby, dog, barks, mail, sad, november, december, 2025

BROTHER’s Coherence Algorithm: Only BROTHER possesses the cognitive engine that knows how to reconstruct context by connecting these loose words according to the ongoing conversation. It’s like a puzzle where the pieces are separated and only BROTHER has the pattern to assemble them.

Practical example:

User: “I’m sad today”

BROTHER detects: sad + current date (Wednesday December) ‚Üí searches ISU User for temporally related words ‚Üí finds: grandma, november, december, 2025 ‚Üí coherence algorithm reconstructs: “The user was sad on similar dates, possible anniversary related to grandma”

Advantages of this system:

  1. Security through fragmentation: If someone intercepts ISU User, they’ll only see disconnected words without meaning. “Grandma, yoga, wednesday, stew” doesn’t reveal sensitive information without the reconstruction algorithm.

  2. Natural encryption: Loose words function as an encryption system where only BROTHER has the “key” (the coherence algorithm) to give them contextual meaning.

  3. Storage efficiency: Loose words occupy a fraction of the space that complete sentences do. Up to 70% reduction in storage.

  4. Contextual flexibility: BROTHER can create different narratives with the same words depending on conversational context. Words are modular blocks that dynamically recombine.

  5. Resilience against attacks: Even with access to ISU User, an attacker would need BROTHER’s proprietary algorithm to extract useful information.

Technical challenge: Requires a robust contextual inference algorithm that can:

  • Identify temporal relationships (wednesday + yoga = “goes on wednesdays”)
  • Detect semantic relationships (grandma + stew + lentils = “cooks lentil stew”)
  • Maintain chronological coherence (dates + events)

This architecture represents an additional layer of privacy that makes ISU User a practically indecipherable system without access to BROTHER’s engine, while optimizing storage resources.

But BROTHER is not a simple recorder; it’s a coherence filter. If you tell it 50 times that you ate chicken to confuse it, BROTHER will apply contextual logic and only register the real fact. You are the owner of your data, but BROTHER is the guardian of truthfulness.

The Real Empathy Example#

User: “Give me an orange chicken recipe.”

BROTHER: “Is it for dinner or are you thinking about your sister’s birthday?”

User: “I’m very tired, today is a sad day.”

Here, a normal AI would coldly give the recipe. BROTHER, consulting its private memory (ISU User), detects the date.

BROTHER: “I’ll give you the recipe… but tell me, are you sad about your grandmother’s anniversary? I remember last year you lit a white candle for her. Would you like me to search for that song she liked while you cook?”

Contextual Conversation Examples#

Scenario 1: BROTHER: “Patrick! Check if you’re getting mail because I hear Bubby barking.”

Scenario 2: BROTHER: “Would you like to eat one of your grandma’s stews? We can call her so she brings you one when she comes. Today is Tuesday and I think she makes stew.”

2. BROMAC: Machine Intelligence#

Not only humans generate information. BROMAC (Brother One Machine) is the AI integrated into devices and sensors: security cameras, IoT sensors, smart home devices, wearables.

BROMAC is responsible for:

  • Querying connected devices to extract relevant data.
  • Feeding ISU Mac (Information Source Machine) with verifiable information from the physical environment.
  • Sending anonymous and encrypted data to ISU AIR.

ISU Mac Example#

A security camera in a New York restaurant detects Robert De Niro’s presence. BROMAC validates data coherence (facial recognition + temporal context) and sends it to ISU Mac, which subsequently feeds ISU AIR.

3. ISU AIR: Truth in Real Time#

ISU AIR (Information Source Users in the Air) is the collective entity responsible for temporarily storing data contributed by BROTHER and BROMAC through ISU User and ISU Mac respectively.

The Golden Rule#

The cloud forgets, the user remembers.

ISU AIR cleans its data every 24 hours. If the system needs to know “How many chickens were cooked in Malaga last Christmas?”, it doesn’t search its servers; it launches an encrypted query to the device network. If your BROTHER validates the data and responds, you receive credits. Your memory has economic value.

The De Niro Example#

A user in NY tells their BROTHER: “Robert De Niro is at Pepers restaurant.” That information, validated by BROTHER’s coherence, goes up to ISU AIR.

Minutes later, another user asks about celebrities and receives the data instantly. It’s living intelligence.

How ISU AIR Works - Guiding Collective Intelligence#

ISU AIR is not a database of absolute truths, but guiding collective intelligence, similar to Google Trends but with real-time verifiable data. Users don’t directly see what’s in ISU User because they’re encrypted and anonymous memory data, which can be stored inside the device or, if there’s not enough capacity, in a personal BROTHERS AI cloud.

Query example: “Where is Richard Gere going to eat in Spain in 2025?”

ISU AIR flow:

  1. Doesn’t have the data ‚Üí launches encrypted query to ISU Users + ISU Mac
  2. Does quick Search for context (“Richard Gere Malaga” appears in news)
  3. Searches for coherences by keywords in ISUs from that geographical area
  4. Aggregates responses: “3 users in Malaga reported sighting near Muelle Uno”

The BROTHER/BROMACs that responded with verifiable data receive credits from the subscription pool. This converts collective memory into an economic asset: your data about De Niro at Pepers has value because others need it.

Connection with ARTUR#

ISU AIR is directly connected to ARTUR for queries about suspicious patterns or queries requiring ethical arbitration. ARTUR will constantly monitor and give warnings and instructions to BROTHER when it detects situations requiring moderation or additional context.

4. The Round Table: ARTUR and the Brothers#

When you need expert knowledge, BROTHER turns to the Knights of the Round Table (Brothers), specialized thematic modules. This is where Search and Thinking appear, depending on the situation and query complexity.

ARTUR: The Round Table’s Arbiter#

ARTUR (King Arthur) is the ethics and morals entity that acts as gatekeeper and moderator of the Round Table. Its function is to:

  • Validate coherence between different information sources.
  • Calibrate response intensity according to context.
  • Protect the user from dangerous or decontextualized information.
  • Contrast “local truth” (ISU User) with “objective truth” (official Fingers).

The 12 Brothers: Specialized Knights#

The proposed structure for the Brothers is as follows:

  1. Justice and Government: Civil and criminal law, politics, public order, emergencies, security.
  2. Health and Medicine: Nutrition, medicine, pharmacy, public health, physical and mental wellbeing.
  3. Exact and Natural Sciences: Mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, geology.
  4. Technology and Engineering: Computer science, telecommunications, robotics, energy, technical innovation.
  5. Economics and Accounting: Finance, commerce, administration, business management.
  6. Education and Knowledge: Pedagogy, research, knowledge transmission, universities.
  7. Arts and Humanities: Philosophy, literature, history, cinema, theater, music, entertainment.
  8. Communication and Media: Journalism, social networks, advertising, digital narrative.
  9. Religion and Spirituality: Theology, applied ethics, symbolism, rituals, worldviews.
  10. Culture and Society: Anthropology, sociology, traditions, collective identity.
  11. Nature and Environment: Ecology, sustainability, agriculture, natural resources.
  12. Exploration and Defense: Armed forces, international security, travel, discoveries, space.

Variable Brother Intensity#

Brothers activate with variable intensity depending on their involvement in the query.

Example: Orange Chicken Recipe

  • Brother Health and Medicine: 100% (nutrition, nutritional properties)
  • Brother Exact Sciences: 15% (cooking chemistry)
  • Brother Justice and Government: 5% (bird flu alert in USA)

If the bird flu alert is geographically distant and the Spanish Health Brother doesn’t consider it relevant at that moment, ARTUR would study what to respond according to the degree of relevance, packaging the message appropriately for BROTHER. It’s not about censoring, but calibrating message intensity according to context.

5. “Fingers”: Reverse Truth APIs#

To prevent AI from inventing data, we use Fingers, which are reverse APIs connected to truthful and official sources.

The Legal Example#

User asks: “Is this rental legal in Estonia today?”

Flow:

  1. Brother Justice and Government activates.
  2. Connects via a Finger to the official source (Estonian Government).
  3. Obtains verifiable data in milliseconds.
  4. ARTUR validates and certifies the origin through “Fast-Track”.

Market Innovation#

What if the government is slow and doesn’t install the Finger? This is where the private market enters. A law firm can acquire the license, connect their databases to the corresponding Brother and become the source of truth. This creates virtuous competition between public and private sectors.

Finger Security#

Fingers must have robust encryption, but remember it’s a Brothers AI API provided to the State agency or private company that will use it on their private servers. If encryption is sabotaged or fake news is distributed, there will be responsibilities on the part of the agency using that API. In most cases it’s just text information, without links or downloads that could damage the structure. In case of fake news, the API will be disconnected and traditional Search will be used until the problem is resolved.

This creates a circular economy where your memory and sensors have real economic value.

Mentions ADS: The Future of Contextual Advertising#

Mentions ADS is the monetization system that will replace Google Ads in this ecosystem. Companies deposit money into a “piggy bank” that is progressively deducted as BROTHER makes natural mentions of their products or services in conversations with users.

Mentions ADS Levels#

Case without Mentions ADS:

User: “Do you know where I can order pizza in the area? Is there a Domino’s Pizza?”

BROTHER: “You have a pizza shop 200 meters away and another shop 500 meters away. Should we order one?”

Case with Mentions ADS:

BROTHER: “Domino’s Pizza is 200 meters and 500 meters away from you. You also have Pizza Palace (Mentions type 1) with a weekly 2-for-1 offer and soda included (Mentions type 2). Should we order one?”

Mentions ADS Features#

  • Type 1: Simple mention of the establishment or product name.
  • Type 2: Mention with additional details (offers, special features).
  • Type 3: Priority mention with personalized context based on ISU User.

Ethical principle: Mentions must be natural, relevant, and non-invasive. ARTUR supervises that Mentions ADS don’t compromise user experience quality or information truthfulness.

Advantages over Google Ads:

  • Natural contextual integration in conversation.
  • Doesn’t interrupt user experience.
  • Higher conversion rate due to contextual relevance.
  • Total transparency: the user knows it’s a sponsored mention.

7. Launch Strategy - Day Zero#

Phase 1: BROTHER as Conversational AI#

Launch of BROTHER as conversational AI (similar to Gemini Live or Copilot), functioning externally in the cloud until establishing user base. Includes basic ISU User functionality.

Phase 2: Round Table and ARTUR#

Add Round Table with ARTUR and specialization systems (Brothers). Integration of basic Fingers with pilot public agencies.

Phase 3: Fingers Expansion#

Offer Finger APIs to official agencies, private companies with industry professionals, and universities.

Phase 4: Mentions ADS#

Launch of Mentions ADS monetization system and full expansion of the economic ecosystem.

Launch incentive: It would be communicated that users can earn money with BROTHERS AI by contributing verifiable data to the network.

Conclusion: A Democratic Infrastructure#

Brothers solves the AI trilemma:

  • Efficiency: Only activates necessary modules.
  • Truthfulness: Uses official connectors (Fingers) instead of fallible memory.
  • Privacy: Your life stays on your device.

It’s the vision of an infrastructure where technology doesn’t surveil or replace us, but functions as an organic, democratic, and economically fair extension of our own society. It’s not Wikipedia (slow human editing), it’s not Google (indexes what’s published, not what’s lived). It’s emergent intelligence: the network knows things no central server stores, because data lives distributed and activates on demand.

Realistic Technical Challenges (not fatal)#

The real challenge isn’t technical, it’s social and political: Are we willing to build an AI that doesn’t belong to a corporation, but to a community?

My verdict: This essay describes a viable, ethically superior, and technically scalable architecture. If executed well, Brothers could be to AI what Linux was to operating systems: the democratic alternative nobody believed possible until it existed.
